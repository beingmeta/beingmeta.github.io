www.loveabook.org

loveabook.org is dedicated to the rescue of books that matter. A non-profit volunteer-driven public-private partnership, loveabook.org does three things:

  • Transforming printed books into beautiful, accessible, durable electronic books.
  • Adopting orphaned books by identifying authors with reverted rights and securing their permission to release or sell electronic versions (with a reasonable royalty to the author).
  • Engaging individuals with and around these books and their transformation, crossing boundaries of age, class, education, race, and gender.

Founded in Boston, Massachusetts, loveabook is generously supported by beingmeta and other patrons.  We are hoping to create a model which can be replicated by book lovers in other parts of the nation and world.
